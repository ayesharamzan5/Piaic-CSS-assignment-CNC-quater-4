PIAIC

PRESIDENTIAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING
Presidential initiative for artificial intellgence and computing (PIAIC) is aprivately funded not. for-profit educational initiative started by president of pakista. Dr.Arif,kazi Rahat Ali and Zia Khan to help promote education , research and business opportunities in emerging tech fields like

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • BlockChain
  • Internet Of Things

Cloud Native Computing:

The cloud computing program prepares the student to program in open source thecnology like,Linux, Docker,Kubernets,javascript,Node.js,React and React Native while introducing the student to development of microservices and software development techniques on cloud platform like AWS

Artificial Inteligence:

The Artificial Inteligence program of PIAIC takes the students through the foundation of the Ai Technology,Via teaching programing in open source language like Python ,Numpy,Pands,Tensorflow 2.0 and (Fabric) and teaching deploying practical AI solutions and use cases, and ending with real-life example of modren day AI in practice.

Blockchain:

The main empasis of Blockchainprogram is to understand the blockchain like Ethereum and hyperledger and learn to program in language such as solidity,javascript on frameworklike Node.js. the program also introduce them to development of distribiuted Apps(DApps), framework like web3 and truffle and an in-depth understanding of Blockchain-as-a-service(BaaS).

INternet of Things

The Internet of Things program intoduced the students to open source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust,WebAssembly,Hyper,Diesel, Google Assistant and Kubernetes While making them efficient in developing Micro Services for IoT Edge,IoT Cloud and voice computing for device control.
